Immersive Tour

Duration: 120 minutes

Want to take an all-inclusive and immersive tour in Kuwait’s prestigious Al Salam Palace? Then this is the tour for you. Our expert guides will take you on a journey through the different stages of Kuwait’s history and allow you to explore all parts of our museum. The journey starts at the story of the palace, which walks you through its different rooms to the vision that made it the museum it is today. The journey continues with the history of the land and the civilizations that were here before us. Finally, your journey is concluded with a one of a kind experience of Kuwait’s History through its 15 rulers showcased for the first time under one roof.
